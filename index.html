ABOUT THE MAP

The first case of COVID-19 in New York State was confirmed on March 1st of 2020 and the state quickly became an epicenter of the pandemic.

New York had the highest number of confirmed cases of any state from the start of the U.S. outbreak until July 22.

This map aims to visualize the rapid spread of Coronavirus throughout New York since March 1st, 2020.

To start using the map, you only need to scroll:

  • Scroll down to forward timeline
  • Scroll up to backtrack timeline
Along with the map, there are 5 charts visualizing the impact of Coronavirus in New York:
  • Total Positive Cases (upper-left)
  • Total Fatal Cases (bottom-left)
  • Total Hospilized Cases (top-right)
  • Categories of Patient Age in Hospital (mid-right)
  • Total Vaccinated (bottom-right)
Note: These charts will automatically appear when scrolling
